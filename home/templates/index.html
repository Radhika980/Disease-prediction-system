HELPFUL LIFESTYLE TIPS

Manage Weight

Losing just 5-7 percent of your body weight can lower your risk for type 2 diabetes.

Eat Healthier

Roast, broil, grill, steam, or bake. Avoid frying. Don't forget that low fat doesn't have to mean low flavor.

Get Active

Get at least 2.5 hours of physical activity every week. Taking 30-minute walks 5 days per week is a good goal.

Get enough sleep

Lack of sleep can impact mood, cognitive function, and physical health.

Stay hydrated

Drinking plenty of water can help maintain energy levels, and promote healthy skin and digestion.

Manage stress

Stress can contribute to numerous health problems, so it's important to find healthy ways to manage stress.
